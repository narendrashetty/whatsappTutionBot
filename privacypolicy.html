Privacy Policy for Tution WhatsApp Bot

Last updated: February 09, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tution WhatsApp Bot's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our educational WhatsApp bot service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information while providing quality tutoring services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User-Provided Information

  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Student name and profile information
  • Academic level and subjects of interest
  • Message content and educational interactions with the bot
  • Assignment submissions and academic queries
  • Learning preferences and study schedules
  • Parent/Guardian contact information (if applicable)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage patterns and learning session durations
  • Performance metrics and progress data
  • Time and frequency of tutoring sessions
  • Device information for optimization
  • WhatsApp API-provided metadata
  • Study pattern analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Delivering personalized tutoring services and educational content
  • Tracking academic progress and generating performance reports
  • Customizing learning paths based on individual needs
  • Sending scheduled reminders for study sessions and assignments
  • Providing feedback on academic performance
  • Improving our tutoring methodologies and content
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing misuse
  • Communicating important updates about our services

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your educational data:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits by certified professionals
  • Multi-factor authentication for data access
  • AWS secure cloud storage with regular backups
  • Automated threat detection and prevention systems
  • Regular security training for our staff

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Educational content providers who enhance our tutoring services
  • Analytics partners who help improve our teaching methods
  • Cloud storage providers with strict security protocols
  • Parents/guardians for student progress monitoring (with consent)
  • Law enforcement agencies when legally required
  • Educational institutions (with explicit permission)

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your complete learning history and personal data
  • Request corrections to your profile information
  • Download your full academic progress reports
  • Opt-out of automated progress tracking
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Restrict data processing for specific purposes
  • Receive your data in a portable format

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for:

  • Active accounts: Full duration of service usage
  • Inactive accounts: 24 months for potential reactivation
  • Academic records: 5 years for reference purposes
  • Financial records: 7 years as per legal requirements
  • Backup data: 30 days rolling backup

8. Children's Privacy

For users under 13 years of age, we require verified parental consent before collecting any personal information. Parents/guardians have the right to review their child's information, request modifications, or deletion of data. We implement additional safeguards for minor students' data protection.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or regulatory requirements. Users will be notified through the WhatsApp bot at least 30 days before any significant changes take effect.

This privacy policy complies with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

These technologies help us understand study patterns and improve our tutoring services. Users can control cookie preferences through their device settings.

Each partner adheres to strict data protection standards and maintains their own privacy policies.
